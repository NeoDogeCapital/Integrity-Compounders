## The Business

Accenture is a global professional services firm providing strategy, consulting, digital, technology, and operations services across 40+ industries. The company operates through three primary segments: Strategy & Consulting (25% of revenue), Technology (45%), and Operations (30%). Revenue is generated through a mix of consulting engagements (time-and-materials), fixed-price contracts, and managed services arrangements.
