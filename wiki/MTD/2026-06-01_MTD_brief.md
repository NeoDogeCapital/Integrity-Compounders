## The Business

Mettler-Toledo manufactures precision instruments for laboratory, industrial, and food retailing applications. The company operates across five segments: Laboratory (largest, ~50% of sales), Industrial, Product Inspection, Food Retail, and Core. Revenue streams include equipment sales (scales, pipettes, titrators, analytical instruments) and recurring revenue from services, consumables, and software. Geographic diversification is strong with approximately 50% of sales from Americas, 30% from Europe, and 20% from Asia/China. The business model benefits from a large installed base requiring calibration, service, and regulatory compliance.

MTD holds a leading position in precision weighing and measurement, with competitive advantages rooted in Swiss engineering precision, regulatory relationships (particularly in pharma and food safety), and switching costs from calibration requirements and workflow integration. Quality gates confirm this: 33.9% ROIC and 27.8% operating margins reflect pricing power and capital efficiency. The company historically generates strong free cash flow and returns capital aggressively through buybacks. Net debt of 1.8x EBITDA is manageable and reflects disciplined M&A and capital allocation.
