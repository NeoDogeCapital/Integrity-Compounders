## The Business

Northrop Grumman is a top-tier aerospace and defense prime contractor focused on space systems, missile defense, autonomous platforms, and cyber/intelligence capabilities. The company operates through four segments: Aeronautics Systems (B-21 Raider stealth bomber, unmanned systems), Defense Systems (missiles, battle management), Mission Systems (sensors, C4ISR), and Space Systems (satellites, launch vehicles, strategic missiles). Unlike peers with commercial aerospace exposure, NOC is pure defense—roughly 85% of revenue comes from U.S. government contracts, with space and classified programs accounting for nearly half of the portfolio.

The competitive advantage rests on technical expertise in classified domains, high barriers to entry in nuclear-capable platforms, and sole-source positions on critical programs like the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) and next-generation space architecture. The company benefits from multi-decade program cycles with embedded relationships across DoD and intelligence agencies. Scale in restricted programs creates switching costs, but this same concentration introduces execution risk and limits margin flexibility on cost-plus contracts.
