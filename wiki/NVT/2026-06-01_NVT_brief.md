## The Business

nVent Electric is a pure-play electrical connection and protection equipment manufacturer serving critical infrastructure, industrial, and commercial construction markets. The company operates through three segments: Enclosures (protecting sensitive electrical components), Electrical & Fastening Solutions (cable management, fastening systems), and Thermal Management (heat tracing and temperature control systems). This is a classic industrial "picks and shovels" business—nVent's products are unsexy but mission-critical components embedded in data centers, energy infrastructure, industrial facilities, and increasingly, electrical vehicle charging and renewable energy installations.

The competitive advantage rests on switching costs and specification-in. Once nVent's enclosures or thermal systems are designed into a facility or industrial process, replacement is expensive and disruptive. The company has dominant share in niche categories—particularly HOFFMAN enclosures and RAYCHEM heat tracing—where reliability trumps price and engineers specify by brand. Distribution relationships with electrical wholesalers create additional moat, though this is a fragmented market where scale matters less than application expertise. Margins at 16.2% operating and 9.9% ROIC suggest a decent but not exceptional business that requires continuous reinvestment.
