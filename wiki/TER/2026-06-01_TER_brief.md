## The Business

Teradyne designs and manufactures automated test equipment (ATE) used to verify the functionality of semiconductors and electronic systems. The company dominates the high-end test market for mobile devices, automotive chips, and increasingly AI processors, with estimated 60%+ share in mobile/compute ATE. Revenue splits roughly 60% semiconductor test, 30% system test (including storage and production boards), and 10% robotics (via Universal Robots and MiR acquisitions). The business operates with capital-light manufacturing through contract partners and maintains high switching costs once integrated into customer production lines.

Competitive advantage stems from technical leadership in complex test solutions—particularly for advanced nodes and high-performance compute—and sticky customer relationships driven by proprietary test cell designs. Major customers include Apple, NVIDIA, Intel, and Samsung, though the business carries inherent cyclicality tied to semiconductor capex cycles. The robotics segment represents a diversification play but remains subscale. Recent AI chip proliferation has created a significant tailwind as hyperscalers and chip designers require sophisticated testing for GPUs and AI accelerators, driving both ASP expansion and volume growth.
