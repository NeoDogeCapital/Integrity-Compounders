## The Business

Viavi Solutions operates in two segments: Network Enablement (NE) and Optical Security & Performance (OSP). The NE segment provides network test, monitoring, and assurance solutions for communications service providers and enterprises deploying fiber, 5G, and cloud infrastructure. OSP manufactures anti-counterfeiting solutions, currency authentication products, and 3D sensing technologies used in consumer electronics. The company generates roughly 65% of revenue from NE and 35% from OSP, with meaningful exposure to telecom capex cycles and consumer device adoption trends.

The competitive advantage here is narrow but real. In network test equipment, Viavi holds market-leading positions in fiber characterization and 5G validation tools, benefiting from sticky customer relationships and the technical complexity of next-generation network rollouts. The OSP business has carved out defensible positions in banknote security features and optical coatings for smartphones. However, both segments face pricing pressure, Chinese competition in lower-end products, and customer concentration risk. ROIC of 23.6% suggests reasonable capital efficiency, though operating margins of 9.0% indicate this isn't a category-defining franchise.
